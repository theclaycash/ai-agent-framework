## Brief

The core business presence for cdiscovery.ai. This project covers the main website (ecommerce + brochure), ongoing content marketing (LinkedIn + blog), GEO strategy, and the technical architecture for Clay's software tools and consulting services.

## Goals

1. Launch a professional web presence with ecommerce for software tools and brochure for consulting services
2. Establish Clay as a trusted voice in ediscovery through consistent LinkedIn and blog content
3. Achieve AI search visibility so ediscovery professionals find Clay's content when asking questions in ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews

## Constraints

- **Timeline:** Ongoing — incremental launches (site V1, then content, then optimization)
- **Budget:** Lean — start with low-cost hosting, scale with revenue
- **Tech:** .NET, Python, Node.js backend. Frontend TBD. Start simple.
- **Compliance:** Content must be accurate for a legal audience. No unverified claims about legal technology capabilities.

## Target Audience

Legal partners responsible for litigation support teams, ediscovery leaders at service providers, directors of legal operations. They care about reducing cost per review, managing data volumes, keeping up with technology changes, retaining good people, and winning more business.
